Metro is a franchise of novels and video games which began with the release of Dimitry Glukhovsky's Metro 2033 novel in 2005.
The books describe Glukhovsky's vision of post-apoclyptic Moscow, describing the consequences of an atomic war: the only survivors strive for existance in the mazes of the Moscow metro system, two decades after the nuclear Holocaust.
